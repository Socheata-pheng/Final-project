Our story

Welcome to Pi-Sa! Nestled in the heart of Phnom Penh, our restaurant celebrates the rich flavors of Khmer cuisine.
Inspired by family recipes and local markets, we offer a warm ambiance with traditional decor
and soothing music. Our menu features the best of Cambodian dishes, crafted with fresh, local ingredients.
Join us for a memorable dining experience that brings people together. Taste the tradition at Pi-Sa!
